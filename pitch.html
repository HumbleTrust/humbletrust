The core issue: trust is not enforced at the infrastructure level. It's optional, self-reported, and easily faked.
02 — Solution
Trust enforced by
smart contract — not promises
  • TrustScore Oracle — on-chain score calculated from LP lock %, creator reputation, trading behaviour, and time. Public, immutable, unfakeable.
  • Bonding Curve with LP Lock Enforcement — liquidity is locked by the smart contract at launch. Creator cannot rug — the contract won't allow it. Automatic Raydium CPMM migration at 50 SOL graduation.
  • Token-2022 Launch Certificates — soulbound NFT issued to every verified launch. Lives on-chain forever. Proves the token went through HumbleTrust verification.
  • Creator Reputation System — on-chain history of every creator's launches, exits, and community score. Bad actors can't hide behind new wallets.
  • Chrome Extension — shows TrustScore on DexScreener, Birdeye, Jupiter, Raydium while users browse. Zero friction for traders.
