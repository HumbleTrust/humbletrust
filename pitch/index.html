Protocol Architecture

Five-Vault Model — total supply enforced on-chain

V2 Token Vaults (1B supply fixed)
🔒
Locked Vault [L]
30–80% of supply
Time-locked. Optional burn-on-unlock (25% or 50%). Cannot touch before lock_days expire.
👤
Creator Vault [C]
0–5% of supply
Vested: 33% day-30, 33% day-60, 34% day-90. Can receive, burn, or add to circulation.
📈
Curve Pool Vault [Q]
25–50% of supply
Active bonding curve pool. Price moves via constant-product (CPMM) or quadratic formula.
🔄
Circulation Vault [R]
15–40% of supply
Public supply. Combined with Curve Vault: Q + R ≥ 50% enforced. Prevents insider concentration.
🎁
Airdrop Vault [A]
0–5% of supply
Monthly epoch distribution (airdrop_amount ÷ 12). Requires min_score ≥ 56 to unlock.
L + C + Q + R + A = 100% · Q + R ≥ 50% · ENFORCED ON-CHAIN
