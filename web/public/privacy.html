Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 31, 2025

HumbleTrust ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome browser extension and website operate with respect to your data.

1. What Data We Collect

The HumbleTrust Chrome extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
  • We do not track your browsing history.
  • We do not store your wallet address or any financial data.

The extension reads token contract addresses from the current web page you are visiting (e.g. DEXScreener, Birdeye, Jupiter) solely to look up the TrustScore for that token.

2. API Requests

When you visit a supported trading platform, the extension sends the token's contract address to the HumbleTrust API (https://humbletrust.vercel.app/api/score) to retrieve its TrustScore. This request contains only the token address — no personal or identifying information is included.

3. Local Storage

The extension may store TrustScore results in your browser's local storage to reduce repeat API calls and improve performance. This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us.

4. Third-Party Services

Our TrustScore calculations may use publicly available on-chain data. We do not share any user data with third parties.

5. Website (humbletrust.vercel.app)

If you sign in using Google OAuth on our website, we receive your email address and public profile information from Google. This is used solely to provide API access and is not shared with third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

HumbleTrust is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the latest revision.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at humble.trust@outlook.com.
